# Projects — Zone Index

with its own `CLAUDE.md`, `TODO.md`, and structure. Projects are NOT
general knowledge — do not link them from `/wiki`.

## Nivel 3 — Per-project indexes

When a project grows past ~10 files, create its own `INDEX.md` at
`projects/<name>/INDEX.md` describing the files in that project.
Update the entry above to link to the per-project index instead of
the `CLAUDE.md` / brief.

Use the template at
[.claude/templates/project-index.md](../.claude/templates/project-index.md)
— copy it, rename to `INDEX.md`, fill in the placeholders.
